OUR ARCHITECTURAL WORK

International Lounge, Delhi International Airport Terminal 3, DIAL

It is the busiest airport in India. Terminal 3 is a two-tier building spread over an area of 20 acres (8.1 ha), with the lower floor being the arrivals area, and the upper floor being a departures area. This terminal has 168 check-in counters, 78 aerobridges at 48 contact stands, 54 parking bays, 95 immigration counters. The plans of the Lounges and Retail were pre approved by the client project .

International Lounge, Delhi International Airport Terminal 3, DIAL

Each level is split into the international and domestic areas. Role and Responsibilities :We were responsible for developing the design, coordinating with other stake holders ,BIM modelling, Clash detection, produce construction documentations, As built drawings, interior refurbishment & site Execution.  

Domestic Lounge, Delhi International Airport, Terminal 3, DIAL

Worked on detailing of Interiors for Retail spaces in Domestic, Air landside terminal. Including on Lease Outline drawing ,MEP coordination and making necessary changes in drawings as per functional requirement suitable.
